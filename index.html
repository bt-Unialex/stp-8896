Game Description

Poly Zen: Puzzle & Art is a visually stunning 3D puzzle game that lets players relax, explore, and challenge their minds through the art of low poly creations.

Rotate, twist, and connect scattered pieces to reveal intricate and beautiful 3D artworks. Every puzzle is a mindful journey, blending creativity with logic, and offering a rewarding escape into the world of digital art. Ideal for puzzle enthusiasts and creative souls, this game brings serenity and challenge in equal measure.
