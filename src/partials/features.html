Main Gameplay Features

  • Rotate to Solve:

    Use intuitive controls to rotate fragments and match shapes.

  • Level Up Gradually:

    Advance from basic to complex models.

  • Visual & Audio Harmony:

    Enjoy a blend of artful visuals and calming soundtracks.

  • Daily Challenges:

    Take on a fresh puzzle every day.

  • Earn Rewards:

    Complete puzzles to unlock new levels and challenges.

  • Discover Hidden Art:

    Each puzzle unlocks a stunning low poly creation.

  • Gallery Mode:

    View and admire all the art you've completed.
