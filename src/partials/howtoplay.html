How to Play

  1. Start a Puzzle:

    Choose from a variety of low poly puzzles.

  2. Rotate & Observe:

    Use touch controls to rotate the pieces.

  3. Match Shapes:

    Align pieces until the full 3D image is revealed.

  4. Unlock New Art:

    Progress to more intricate models with every success.

  5. Daily Puzzles:

    Choose from a variety of low poly puzzles.

  6. Relax & Enjoy:

    Take your time and enjoy the calming journey.
